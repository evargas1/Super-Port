About

After graduating High School and getting a taste of coding in my computer science classes, I was positive that I wanted to pursue a career in Web Development. I did thorough research into bootcamps and unfortunately was unable to attend any because at the time I was only 16. I was not giving up. I sought out mentors in the industry and they encouraged me to become a self taught developer. Just the thought of teaching myself something as "complex" as coding was terrifying. Even so, the thought of walking away without even trying was far more petrifying. So here I am now.
